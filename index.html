About

Hello!

I'm Priscilla, an innovative engineer, creator, and adventurer recently graduated from USC and living in Los Angeles.

I'm a mechanical engineer and freelance photographer with a love for finding the intersection of design and functionality. I love making things from products to any form of media. Passionate about education, making an impact on the community, and women in tech.

I spend a lot of time hiking up mountains, collecting patches and pins for my jean jacket, and bullet journaling.
Always excited to chat about anything!
