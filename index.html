About

Hello!

I'm Priscilla, an innovative engineer, creator, and adventurer living in Los Angeles.

I'm an aspiring mechanical engineer with a love for finding the intersection of design and functionality. I love making things from products to any form of media. Passionate about education, making an impact on the community, and women in tech.
